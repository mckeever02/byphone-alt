ByPhone

Phones

You have two options for making your calls:
a Snom deskphone, a free downloadable softphone.

Pre-configured deskphones

We offer a range of Snom phones to suit every need. If you order the phones from Byphone, they will be pre-configured so you can just plug them in and start using them with our service straight away. If you purchase them directly, we will forward you the configuration settings so that you can get started.

Snom D710

The D710 is a state-of-the-art, full-featured IP desk phone for small or large scale deployments. With five programmable LED keys built in for visual call indication alerts, the D710 blends an elegant design for high usability with high performance and affordability. The D710 delivers a rich set of business telephony features critical to todays real-time, information-driven business workforce. Built for HD audio, it provides superior, crystal-clear end-to-end call quality.

Snom 320

Ideal for the office and everyone who spends a lot of time on the phone, the Snom 320 is an affordable, yet powerful SIP business phone with built-in, full- duplex speakerphone and three party conference bridging. A 2 x 24 semi-graphic LC display and menu-driven user interface support an uncomplicated feature management. 12 programmable keys with LEDs support quick access to important extensions. Call list and address book can contain up to 100 entries each, and entries can be added and changed easily. Other features like blocking anonymous calls, downloading ringtones, auto-answer mode, DND mode are only examples from an abundance of functions which make phone use convenient and comfortable while increasing productivity.

Snom D725

The D725 has 18 programmable keys with multi- coloured LEDs fir visual call indication providing users increased visibility and efficiency. The USB port opens the D725 up to a choice of functionality enhancing features including use of a USB corded or Bluetooth headset, connection to the D7 expansion keypad or WiFi. The D725 features a Gigabit Ethernet switch to meet the most demanding telephony requirements. The advanced speaker and microphone system and the Digital Signal Processor (DSP) ensure the D725 delivers a cutting-edge audio performance.

Snom D7

The D7 expansion module provides users who experience high call coverage with greater flexibility and increased productivity. The D7 expansion module enhances the 700 series by adding 18 programmable keys with LEDs to deliver features such as line, speed dial, busy lamp field and presence indication. The D7 connects to the telephone using a USB cable, and up to 3 expansion modules can be daisy chained to add 54 function keys.

Get started out of the box with a softphone

A downloadable pre-configured soft phone that will work on any smart phone, tablet or PC. You don't even need a physical deskphone to start using the service. Download a free softphone and get started straight away. After you've set up your new Byphone phone system we'll email you the neccessary settings to get you started on your softphone.
